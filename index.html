Apex Legends

Apex Legends is an online multiplayer battle royale game featuring squads of three players using pre-made characters with distinctive abilities, called "Legends", similar to those of hero shooters. Alternate modes have been introduced allowing for single and for two-player squads since the game's release.

How To Play Apex Legends

  • Learn the best way to drop
  • Communicate with your squad
  • Fill up your inventory and swap it often.
  • Keep your health up and shield charged.
  • Learn to move fast
  • Always have grenades on hand.
  • Stick with your squad and stay in the Ring
  • Focus on playing as two to three Legends at first.
